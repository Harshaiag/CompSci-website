Welcome to LeicesterCS!
LeicesterCS is a vibrant and inclusive society at the University of Leicester, dedicated to fostering a thriving community for students interested in computer science, software engineering, and related disciplines. Whether you're a seasoned coding enthusiast or just starting your journey into the world of technology, LeicesterCS is the perfect place to connect with like-minded individuals, expand your knowledge, and have a fantastic time along the way.

Membership in LeicesterCS is open to all students at the University of Leicester, regardless of their level of experience or degree program. Whether you're studying computer science, software engineering, or any other field with an interest in technology, we welcome you to join our community. Membership offers a range of benefits, including exclusive access to events, networking opportunities, and a platform to collaborate with peers on exciting projects.

Get Involved:
Join LeicesterCS today and become part of a vibrant community that embraces curiosity, collaboration, and innovation. Follow us on social media, attend our events, and get involved in shaping the future of LeicesterCS.
We look forward to meeting you and embarking on this exciting journey together!
